About National Zoological Park

Our Mission

National Zoological Park is dedicated to wildlife conservation, education, and providing exceptional experiences for our visitors. We house over 2,000 animals representing 200 species in habitats designed to mimic their natural environments.

Our commitment extends beyond the zoo walls, with active participation in global conservation programs and breeding initiatives for endangered species. We believe in inspiring the next generation of conservationists through immersive educational programs.

Conservation

Active in 15+ global conservation programs protecting endangered species worldwide.

Education

Educational programs for 50,000+ students annually, fostering environmental stewardship.
